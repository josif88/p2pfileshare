About P2P LAN File Share

The Problem: Have you ever needed to send a file from your phone to a Smart TV, interactive board, or a colleague's laptop, but didn't have a cable, USB drive, or an easy way to connect them? Emailing large files is clunky, and cloud uploads are too slow.

The Solution: This Zero-Config P2P File Share tool solves that instantly. Whether you are an IT professional in a fast-paced business environment, or just sharing family videos at home, this app allows devices on the same local network (LAN or Wi-Fi) to discover each other automatically.

  • Fast & Direct: Files are transferred peer-to-peer (P2P) directly between devices via WebRTC. No intermediate servers or cloud storage involved.
  • Secure & Private: Your files never leave your local network.
  • Unlimited Size: Send large files instantly without artificial size restrictions.

⚠️ Important: Please ensure both devices have this application open in their browser to discover each other and transfer files successfully.
